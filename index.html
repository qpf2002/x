A unique experience as an
exchange student in Lisbon

Have you ever wanted to participate in an Exchange Program during your studies? If you have, Lisbon is a must among the cities you should be considering as your destination. From its breathtaking and colourful streets to its awe-inspiring beaches and the most welcoming people, Lisbon is the perfect city to have one of the most amazing and unforgettable experiences of your life!

The city and its
marvelous appeal

Lisbon is a wonderful city, full of activities to enjoy every day, not only for tourists in general, but especially for exchange students, which always come in never-ending numbers each semester. Make sure to participate in these activities, as it will not only allow you to explore the beauty of Lisbon, but will help you meet new people as well!

Never-ending fun
and endless nights

Lisbon offers activities for everyone, whether you prefer daytime or nighttime. From karaoke nights and the most fun night clubs to surf lessons and the most mesmerising libraries, you will never have a chance to get bored in this city.
